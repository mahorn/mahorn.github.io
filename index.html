United States Earthquakes

This map shows earthquakes within each region from 1933 to 2014 in the United States. A slider was created in order to visualize earthquakes by year of occurrence.

This data was obtained from Strong-Motion Virtual Data Center (VDC).

It was built using custom JavasCript code drawing upon the Cartodb, Simple Statistics and Toposon Javascript Libraries.

  • See the map: United States Earthquakes
  • Data Source: Strong-Motion Virtual Data Center United States Topojson

United States Electric Power Generation

This map shows Hydro, Petroleum and Solar electric power generation across the United States.

Upon clicking on the map, you will see all the electric power plants within 500 km and the area will be highlighted. Information regarding individual plants like its name, capacity in Megawatts and its distance from the plant to the click point will be also available.

It was built using custom JavasCript code drawing upon the Leaflet JavaScript Library.

  • See the map: United States Electric Power Generation
  • Data Source: U.S Energy Information Administration

Kentucky eBird Observations and Audubon Important Bird and Biodiversity Areas, 2014

This Map shows bird observations of Kentucky Imperiled/Vulnerable species from 2014 eBird observation records. Kentucky and Global ranking statuses were obtained from the Kentucky State Nature Preserve Commission's published county records (http://naturepreserves.ky.gov/pubs/Pages/cntyreport.aspx).

The eBird observation records were obtained in May 2015 from Cornell's eBird website. Species without special State or Global ranking status were excluded from the analysis.

This map was generated in QGIS using CartoDB CSS styles.

  • See the map: Kentucky eBird Observations and Audubon Important Bird and Biodiversity Areas, 2014
  • Data Source: Nature Preserve

Cancer Incidence in the United States

This map shows the average incidence rate per 100,000 people, average from 2008 - 2012.

Data were downloaded from the National Cancer Institute on 11/13/12015. The data describe cancer rates for all cancer sites by mortality, for all races, sexes, and ages, averaged over the years of 2008 - 2012.

This map was generated in QGIS using CartoDB CSS styles.

  • See the map: Cancer Incidence in the United States
  • Data Source: National Cancer Institute
